What this data is telling you

In the bar chart, you can see the quality of life index and the safety index in a couple of European countries. What's interesting is that they don't seem to be related.

If you click on a country's name, you'll see a line representing the purchasing power index in the past four years. The purchasing power seems to be related to the quality of life in a country.
